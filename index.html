Hi, My name is Hanrui (Raye) Zhang.

I'm a third-year PhD candidate in Department Computational Medicine and Bioinformatics, the Univeristy of Michigan. I have been working with Dr. Yuanfang Guan on developing various machine learning algorithms for digital health and biomedical applications.

My previous works includes the digial diagnose of Parkinson's disease, Sleep Apnea, and untilization of Electronic Health Records (EHR). Currently my research has been focus on the prediction of cancer drug response based on high-throughput drug screening and the potential clinical appilcations.
